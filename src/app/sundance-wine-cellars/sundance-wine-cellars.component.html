Sundance Wine Cellars

Sundance Wine Cellars is one of the oldest wine retailers in the Willamette Valley, hosting an extensive, well-curated selection of wines from Oregon, the Northwest, and around the world. We are located in Eugene, Oregon - a short drive from many of Oregon's finest vineyards.

Our location, in Oregon wine country, combined with long-standing relationships with many winemakers, makes it possible for us to bring you small production wines, reserve bottlings and special cuvees that are unavailable elsewhere. We spend as much time as we can tasting wines and talking with winemakers in order to stay on top of the ever changing Oregon and Northwest wine scenes, so when you call or email us, you will get the straight scoop - not only what to buy, but when to drink it and more.

A Selection From Around the World

We offer a wide selection of rare and hard to find wines from all over the world. While our focus is primarily on locally produced wines from here in the Willamette Valley, we also offer a diverse selection of wines from all of the other major wine producing regions in the world. Give us a call at 541-687-9463, as there is a good chance we might have it.

We have the largest selection of wine in all of Oregon and the largest selection of Oregon wines in the world, with over 500 different Oregon Pinot Noirs in our shop, an excellent selection of high-end Washington and California wines, as well as wines from Bordeaux, Burgundy, Italy and around the world.

Events & Tastings

We host free themed tastings every Friday and Saturday from 5PM - 7PM, showcasing various wine regions on Fridays and hosting local and regional wineries on Saturdays. Our tasting bar is always open and offers free samples of many of our featured wines.
