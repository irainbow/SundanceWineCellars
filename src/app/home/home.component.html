Hundreds of Oregon Pinot Noirs

Sundance Wine Cellars, the “parent” business of Oregon Wine Merchants, began in 1983 and currently houses the Northwest’s largest selection of wine, including over 500 different Oregon Pinot Noirs. In addition to the Pinot Noirs we have on hand, we have access to hundreds more through our network of long-established relationships with Oregon wineries. Please email, text or call us with any inquiries as to the availability of particular wines.

A Selection From Around the World

We offer a wide selection of rare and hard to find wines from all over the world. While our focus is primarily on locally produced wines from here in the Willamette Valley, we also offer a diverse selection of wines from all of the other major wine producing regions in the world. Give us a call at 541.687.9463, as there is a good chance we might have it.
