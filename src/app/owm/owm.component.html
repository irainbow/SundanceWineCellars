Oregon Wine Merchants

Oregon Wine Merchants is your online resource for accessing hard-to-find and allocated Oregon Pinot Noir. The “parent” business of Oregon Wine Merchants, Sundance Wine Cellars, began in 1983 and currently houses the Northwest’s largest selection of wine, including over 500 different Oregon Pinot Noirs. In addition to the Pinot Noirs we have on hand, we have access to hundreds more through our network of long-established relationships with Oregon wineries. Please email or call us with any inquiries as to the availability of particular wines.
